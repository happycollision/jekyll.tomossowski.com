Tom Ossowski is a stage director and music director. He teaches in the BFA Music Theatre Program at Florida State University, is the Producing Artistic Director for the Post Playhouse at Fort Robinson State Park in Nebraska, and is on the faculty at The Central Academy of Drama in Beijing, China.
